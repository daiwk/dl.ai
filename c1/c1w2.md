## logistic regression

given $x$, want $\hat y=P(y=1|x), x \in R^{n_x}$

params: $w \in R^{n_x}, b \in R$

output: $\hat y= \sigma(w^Tx+b), \sigma(z)=\frac{1}{1+e^(-z)}$
